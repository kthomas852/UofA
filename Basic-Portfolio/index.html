About Me

Starting from an early age I have been captivated with technology and understanding the interworking's of the many complicated machines around me. Out of High School I had a Strong interest in digital design. After dabbling in graphic arts I graduated with a Bachelor's degree focused in Geography and GIS from The University of New Mexico. My first professional experience working the engineering space was as an electrical engineering tech at Bladewerx, a research and development company that specialized in radiation detection and spectroscopy. The need to be in the dot com sphere and a love for local businesses brought me to my current position at Yelp. My goal is to further my development and launch my career anew, working as a fullstack developer.
